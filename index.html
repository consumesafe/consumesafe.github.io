Consume Safe

An App that acts as a Brand WatchDog for consumer products across the market

  • The federal government agency for Consumer Safety maintains a data portal that registers and verifies complaints, incidents and recalls related to various industrial products.

  • As the portal is not directly connected to consumers, the time lost between incident acknowledgement, verification, recall notification and actual off-loading from the shelves costs much damage to the consumers, even after the first instance of a clear product flaw.

  • To facilitate a medium of communication that publishes, government data more socially and gives the information at the fingertips of the consumers is the need of the hour!

About

  • ConsumeSafe is an App that acts as a Brand WatchDog for consumer products across the market.

  • The app’s main goal is instant communication, getting the message across the consumers, regarding a particular product incident, recall or safety notification.

  • The app notifies its users about product notifications based on their interests, previous purchase history or based on a browsing history.

  • The app also acts as a preventive measure to spread awareness quickly before the products get recalled from the shelf.

The app acts on the input from the US Consumer Product Safety Commision, a federal agency that publishes the data on its “SaferProducts” api https://www.saferproducts.gov/

Features

For Consumers

  • Instant notifications in the mobile and web app

  • Watch brands, register your products and get notified for recalls.

  • Report new incidents to US CPSC from anywhere using Mobile App

For Businesses

  • Instant notifications in the mobile and web app regarding new incident reports

  • Notify consumers and acknowledge public safety notifications

  • Take rapid decisions regarding product replacement

Visual dashboards to summarize the data for both consumers and businesses
